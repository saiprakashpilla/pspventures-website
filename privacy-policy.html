Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 20, 2026

1. Information We Collect

At PSP Ventures, we build our applications and digital services to prioritize on-device storage. When you visit our website or use our applications, we may process:

  • Inquiry Data: Information you submit voluntarily via forms (e.g., name, email, subject, text message).
  • Device Telemetry: Anonymized usage data, loading latency metrics, browser metadata, and screen sizes to improve app layouts.
  • Local Sync Keys: Authentication markers to sync configurations across desktop and mobile devices.

2. How We Use Information

We process your data to answer partnership applications, optimize load latencies, compile app store compliance metrics, and ensure your local workspace setups sync securely. We do not sell, trade, or distribute your email addresses or contact profiles to marketing affiliates.

3. On-Device Storage Philosophy

For our SaaS and AI tools (such as ScribeAI and Taskify), we focus on edge computing and local-first databases. Your markdown check-lists and transcript profiles remain stored inside your browser sandboxes or local operating system directories unless you activate cloud syncing channels.

4. Data Rights

Under the GDPR and CCPA, you have full authority to request access to, correction of, or deletion of any contact histories submitted to us. Please direct any such requests to our verification and privacy desk at privacy@psp.company.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our privacy rules occasionally. We advise users to review this page periodically to keep informed of policy modifications.
