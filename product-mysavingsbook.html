My Savings Book

Simple Personal Finance Management

My Savings Book is a security-first expense manager app that runs on Android. It allows users to register daily income, categorize custom spending parameters, and observe monthly savings indicators on a sleek dark-themed dashboard.

Operating entirely offline by default, the app ensures that your financial logs stay compiled locally on your device storage. Security is baked in without cloud data leaks.

Key Benefits & Features

  • Instant Entry Logs: Quick logging mechanism to write inputs in under 3 seconds.
  • Luminous Visual Charts: Observe visual asset splits and spending limits indicators instantly.
  • Zero-Ad Privacy: Free of external ad-tracking systems, keeping financial summaries protected.
